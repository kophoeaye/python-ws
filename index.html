Welcome to Asian Culture & History

Asia, the largest continent in the world, is home to a mosaic of cultures, languages, and traditions that have shaped human civilization for thousands of years. From the ancient dynasties of China and the empires of India to the cultural heritages of Japan, Korea, and Southeast Asia, this site celebrates the stories that define Asia’s past and present.

Regional Heritage

East Asia

Includes China, Japan, Korea, and Mongolia — known for philosophies like Confucianism and Taoism, as well as advances in art, science, and governance.

South Asia

Home to India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, and Nepal — birthplace of major religions such as Hinduism, Buddhism, and Sikhism.

Southeast Asia

A region of cultural fusion between India, China, and indigenous traditions — including Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, and the Philippines.

Central & West Asia

The crossroads of cultures through the Silk Road — blending Persian, Arab, Turkish, and nomadic influences.

Cultural Traditions

Asian traditions encompass a wide range of festivals, cuisines, and artistic expressions. Festivals like Diwali, Chinese New Year, and Songkran reflect ancient values of community, family, and renewal.
